Fascinating Places to Visit

Very fascinating places in the world that are worth a visit!

Gilgit-Baltistan, Pakistan

Gilgit-Baltistan is home mountain peaks over 20,000 feet, including the renowned K-2. Shandur, the world’s highest polo ground, is also located here, as is the turquoise-hued Attabad Lake in Hunza Valley.

Serengeti National Park

Located in northern Tanzania, the 5,700-square-mile wildlife park is home to The Great Migration – the annual movement of millions of animals in search of food and breeding grounds.

Great Barrier Reef

It's one of the Seven Natural Wonders of the World, stretching nearly 1,500 miles along the eastern coastline of Australia and featuring 3,000 coral reefs, 600 islands and more than 1,600 species of fish.

Bali, Indonesia

This Indonesian island offers some of the best beaches in the world, along with volcanic mountains, elephant sanctuaries and a wealth of five-star resorts.

Santorini, Greece

The white and pastel-hued villages dotting the coastline of this Greek island seem to be straight out of a postcard. Shorelines are sprinkled with colorful beaches, including black sands at Kamari Beach.

Dubai, United Arab Emirates

Dubai is home to a bevy of superlatives, including the tallest building in the world (Burj Khalifa at 2,717 feet), the world's largest indoor theme park (IMG Worlds of Adventure), the largest man-made marina in the world and the world's first rotating skyscraper, set to open in 2020.

Sydney, Australia

Part laid-back beach town, part sprawling metropolis and part cultural retreat, Sydney has many facets that please a variety of travelers.

Prague, Czech Republic

Gothic structures stretching from the Old Town Square to Prague Castle (the largest coherent castle complex in the world) will make you feel as if you're in a fairy tale. You'll also want to walk across the Charles Bridge and make time to marvel at the Prague Astronomical Clock.

Phuket, Thailand

The largest island in Thailand boasts enough marvels to impress any traveler. You'll find wide sandy beaches, limestone caves and distinctive rock formations. If the scenery doesn't amaze you, then the lavish spas, upscale dining, fine art galleries and swanky hotels just might.
